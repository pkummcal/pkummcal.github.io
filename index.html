Postdoctoral Fellows

The MMCAL group at PKU seeks a top postdoctoral researcher proficient in (1) multimedia coding and processing, (2) 3D vision and multimodal learning (e.g. 3D point cloud/image/video coding, quality assessment and enhancenment, 3D Gaussian Splatting, generative AI, world model, and multimodal large model, etc.).
