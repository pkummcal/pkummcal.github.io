Biography

Dr. Wei Gao is currently an Assistant Professor (Ph.D. Supervisor) with the School of Electronic and Computer Engineering, Peking University (PKU), Shenzhen, China. He is now leading the Immersive Video Perception (IVP) Group. He received the Ph.D. degree in Computer Science with the Department of Computer Science, City University of Hong Kong (CityU), Kowloon, Hong Kong. He was a Camera ISP Engineer at OmniVision Technologies (OVTI), Shanghai, China. He was a Visiting Scholar at the Electrical Engineering Department, University of California, Los Angeles (UCLA), CA, USA. He was a Postdoctoral Fellow and Research Associate at the Department of Computer Science, City University of Hong Kong (CityU), Kowloon, Hong Kong, and a Research Fellow at the School of Electrical and Electronic Engineering, Nanyang Technological University (NTU), Singapore, respectively. His research interests include perceptual image/video/point cloud coding (e.g., information theory and interpretable neural network for compression with emphasis on human and machine vision perception), image/video/point cloud restoration (e.g., inverse problem in mathematics and advanced models), and salient/3D object detection (e.g., visual attention and perception with emphasis on physiology and psychology), etc. Moreover, pure theoretical research on applied mathematics and optimization, visual perception, aritifical intelligence and practical information systems are also very interesting. He believes that: good innovations should have good explainable theories.
