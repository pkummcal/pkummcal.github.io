Research Projects

1. Rate-Distortion Optimized Multimedia Coding and Processing

The research work includes 3D point cloud coding, image/video coding, multimodal coding, quality enhancement, and the related standardization efforts. We mainly focus on advanced deep learning solutions for coding optimization, and devise various methods to obtain better rate-distortion performance with flexibility and scalability. We have recently published high quality papers on IEEE TIP, IEEE TCSVT, IEEE TMM, IEEE TGRS, ACM MM, CVPR, AAAI, DCC, etc., and participated into the standardization work of MPEG, AVS, and IEEE. The monograph titled “Point Cloud Compression: Technologies and Standardization” has been published by Springer Nature in 2024.

4. Immersive Media and Autonomous Driving Applications

Building upon the above research projects, we are further investigating the related technologies to promote the practical applications of immersive media and autonomous driving, including multimodal data compression, quality assessment and enhancement, real-time implementations, generative AI and world model, multimodal large model-based perception and decision, etc. We also have close collaborations with several renowned high-tech companies. The aim of this research project is propelling the next generation innovations of immersive media and autonomous driving to benefit the human society.
